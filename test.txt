Bidhannagar Sector V
Feb 17, 2023
Harassment
Description:
        The victim, a 21-year-old IT intern working in a private tech firm, had stayed late at the office to complete a pending assignment. Her project manager offered to drop her home, stating it was unsafe to travel alone at night. Instead of taking the main route, he diverted the car to a deserted area near the Salt Lake stadium and made inappropriate advances toward her. The victim managed to send her live location to a friend who alerted the police. The Accused, Ramesh Nair, originally from Kochi, Kerala, has been detained for questioning and the firm has been notified of the incident.

--Staff Correspondent via EasternChronicle News, Feb 18
